void IDamageable.OnDamageTaken ( float  damageTaken)

OnDamageTaken required that the implementor be able to respond to a float amount of damage taken.

Usually this is by decrementing a health variable found in the implementing script, then destroying the implementing object when their health is equal to or less than 0.

Implemented in Unit, Building, and HeroUnit.
